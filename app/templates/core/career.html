Introduction

I initially studied as a Chemical Engineer to MEng level before pursuing a Ph.D researching optimal approaches to the production of anti-malarial drug treatments. During this time, I was highly active in the research community, in addition to consulting for around a dozen industrial manufacturers around the globe. I eventually left the Ph.D position in order to pursue my career in Data Science. Below is a summary of my qualifications, publications and conference presentations.
